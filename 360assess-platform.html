Emma, your 360° results reveal a leader with exceptional execution capabilities and genuine people commitment — consistently recognized by all rater groups. Your most significant development opportunity lies in Strategic Visibility: while you score highly on doing strategic work, peers and your manager indicate you communicate strategic thinking less clearly than your actual capability warrants.

✦ TOP STRENGTHS
1. Results Orientation — rated 4.6/5 by all groups. Consistent, reliable, high-output delivery recognized universally.
2. Inclusive Leadership — direct reports rate you 4.8/5. Psychological safety scores are in the top 12% of global benchmark.
3. Stakeholder Commitment — peers note above-average follow-through and responsiveness.
⚠ DEVELOPMENT AREAS
1. Strategic Communication — self vs. peer gap of 0.8pts. You may be doing the thinking but not narrating it publicly.
2. Delegation Confidence — manager rates autonomy-granting 0.6pts below team average for your level.

📅
Days 1–30: Begin a "Strategy Brief" habit — a 5-sentence weekly written summary of your team's strategic contributions, shared with your manager.
📅
Days 31–60: Volunteer to present 1 strategic initiative in the monthly leadership forum. Request feedback from 2 senior peers afterward.
📅
Days 61–90: Delegate one project component fully to a senior team member. Document the outcome and reflect in your next 1:1 with your manager.
